Get to know me

I'm from Montreal, Canada and I've always loved being creative. I grew up being a competitive figure skater which helped teach me how to express myself artistically. I originally chose my career path in kinesiology to help others reach their personal fitness goals. I loved building strong interpersonal connections with my clients but felt I was lacking my creative side. I then discovered user experience and web design which allows me to transfer my client-centred problem solving skills to create holistic, accessible and meaningful digital products for everyday people.
